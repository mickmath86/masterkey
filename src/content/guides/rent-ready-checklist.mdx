# The Ultimate Rent-Ready Checklist

Getting your rental property ready for new tenants involves more than just cleaning and minor repairs. This comprehensive checklist ensures your property meets all safety, habitability, and legal requirements while creating an attractive living space that commands top rental rates.

## Physical Property Inspection

### Exterior Assessment

**Structural Elements:**
<CheckCircleIcon />
- Roof inspection for leaks, missing shingles, or damage
- Gutters and downspouts properly attached and functional
- Foundation free of cracks or water damage
- Siding, brick, or exterior walls in good condition
- Windows and doors properly sealed and operational

**Landscaping and Grounds:**
- Lawn mowed and edged professionally
- Shrubs and trees trimmed and maintained
- Walkways and driveways clean and safe
- Outdoor lighting functional and adequate
- Fencing and gates secure and in good repair

**Safety and Security:**
- All exterior locks rekeyed with new keys
- Deadbolts installed on all exterior doors
- Window locks functional on all accessible windows
- Adequate exterior lighting for safety
- House numbers clearly visible from street

### Interior Systems Check

**Electrical Systems:**
- All outlets tested and functional
- GFCI outlets in bathrooms, kitchen, and outdoor areas
- Light fixtures and switches operational
- Electrical panel labeled and accessible
- No exposed wiring or electrical hazards

**Plumbing Systems:**
- All faucets, toilets, and fixtures functional
- Water pressure adequate throughout property
- Hot water heater operational and recently serviced
- No leaks in pipes, fixtures, or appliances
- Proper drainage in all sinks, tubs, and showers

**HVAC Systems:**
- Heating and cooling systems professionally serviced
- New air filters installed
- Thermostat functional and programmed
- Ductwork clean and properly sealed
- Adequate ventilation in all rooms

## Safety and Code Compliance

### Life Safety Systems

**Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detection:**
<CheckCircleIcon />
- Smoke detectors in all required locations per local code
- Carbon monoxide detectors near sleeping areas and fuel-burning appliances
- All detectors tested with fresh batteries
- Interconnected system if required by local code
- Tenant education materials provided

**Fire Safety:**
- Fire extinguisher accessible (if required)
- Clear egress paths from all rooms
- Window escape routes unobstructed
- Emergency contact information posted
- Fire escape plan available for multi-story properties

**Security Measures:**
- All locks changed since previous tenant
- Security system functional (if applicable)
- Peepholes or security cameras at entrances
- Adequate lighting in all common areas
- Emergency lighting in stairwells and exits

### Building Code Compliance

**Habitability Standards:**
- Adequate heating for all living spaces
- Proper ventilation and air circulation
- Sufficient natural light in living areas
- Waterproof barriers in bathrooms and kitchens
- Proper insulation for energy efficiency

**Accessibility Considerations:**
- ADA compliance for applicable properties
- Handrails on all staircases
- Non-slip surfaces in bathrooms
- Adequate door widths for accessibility
- Proper lighting for safety and visibility

## Deep Cleaning and Maintenance

### Room-by-Room Cleaning

**Kitchen:**
- All appliances cleaned inside and out
- Cabinets and drawers wiped down and organized
- Countertops sanitized and polished
- Sink and faucet descaled and shining
- Floor mopped and baseboards cleaned

**Bathrooms:**
- Toilets, tubs, and showers deep cleaned and sanitized
- Mirrors and fixtures polished
- Grout cleaned and sealed if necessary
- Exhaust fans cleaned and functional
- Fresh caulking around tubs and showers

**Living Areas:**
- Carpets professionally cleaned or replaced
- Hardwood floors refinished if needed
- Walls washed and touch-up paint applied
- Light fixtures cleaned and bulbs replaced
- Windows cleaned inside and out

**Bedrooms:**
- Closets cleaned and organized
- Carpet or flooring in excellent condition
- Walls and ceilings free of marks or damage
- Adequate lighting and electrical outlets
- Window treatments clean and functional

### Appliance and Fixture Maintenance

**Kitchen Appliances:**
- Refrigerator cleaned, serviced, and temperature-tested
- Stove and oven cleaned and safety-checked
- Dishwasher cleaned and cycle-tested
- Garbage disposal cleaned and operational
- All appliances have current manuals available

**Laundry Equipment:**
- Washer and dryer cleaned and tested
- Lint traps cleaned and exhaust vents clear
- Water connections checked for leaks
- Electrical connections safe and proper
- Operating instructions provided

## Legal Documentation and Paperwork

### Required Disclosures

**Federal Requirements:**
<CheckCircleIcon />
- Lead paint disclosure (pre-1978 properties)
- EPA renovation, repair, and painting rule compliance
- Fair housing information provided
- Mold and environmental hazard disclosures

**State and Local Disclosures:**
- Property condition disclosure statements
- Previous flood or water damage history
- Registered sex offender database information
- Rent control or stabilization notices
- Utility responsibility disclosures

### Lease Documentation

**Lease Agreement Preparation:**
- Current lease template with all required clauses
- Property-specific addendums and rules
- Pet policy and fee structure
- Maintenance responsibility matrix
- Emergency contact procedures

**Move-In Documentation:**
- Detailed move-in inspection checklist
- Photo documentation of property condition
- Key and access card inventory
- Utility transfer instructions
- Tenant handbook with policies and procedures

## Marketing and Presentation

### Professional Photography

**Interior Shots:**
- Wide-angle photos of all rooms
- Natural lighting whenever possible
- Staged furniture and decor (if applicable)
- Clean, clutter-free spaces
- High-resolution images for online listings

**Exterior Photography:**
- Curb appeal shots from multiple angles
- Parking areas and outdoor spaces
- Neighborhood context and amenities
- Seasonal considerations for best presentation
- Drone photography for unique properties

### Property Listing Preparation

**Accurate Descriptions:**
- Precise square footage and room counts
- Complete amenity and feature list
- Neighborhood highlights and conveniences
- Transportation and school information
- Pet policies and restrictions clearly stated

**Competitive Pricing:**
- Recent market analysis completed
- Comparable property research
- Seasonal pricing considerations
- Incentive packages if needed
- Flexible lease terms evaluation

## Final Inspection and Certification

### Professional Inspections

**Required Certifications:**
- Electrical system safety inspection
- Plumbing system functionality test
- HVAC system performance verification
- Smoke and carbon monoxide detector testing
- Lead paint inspection (if applicable)

**Optional but Recommended:**
- Professional cleaning verification
- Pest control inspection and treatment
- Energy efficiency assessment
- Security system functionality test
- Landscaping and exterior maintenance review

### Move-In Ready Verification

**Final Walkthrough Checklist:**
- All repairs and maintenance completed
- Property thoroughly cleaned and sanitized
- All systems tested and functional
- Keys and access devices prepared
- Welcome package and documentation ready

**Tenant Preparation:**
- Utility setup instructions provided
- Emergency contact information posted
- Property management contact details available
- Move-in appointment scheduled
- Security deposit and first month's rent collected

## Ongoing Maintenance Schedule

### Preventive Maintenance Program

**Monthly Tasks:**
- HVAC filter replacement
- Smoke detector battery testing
- Exterior walkthrough inspection
- Tenant satisfaction check-in
- Maintenance request review

**Quarterly Tasks:**
- Deep cleaning of common areas
- Gutter cleaning and inspection
- Appliance maintenance and service
- Landscaping and exterior upkeep
- Lease compliance review

**Annual Tasks:**
- Professional HVAC system service
- Roof and structural inspection
- Paint touch-ups and cosmetic repairs
- Lease renewal negotiations
- Market rent analysis and adjustment

## Common Rent-Ready Mistakes

### Avoid These Oversights

**Incomplete Cleaning:**
- Rushing the deep cleaning process
- Missing hidden areas like inside appliances
- Neglecting outdoor spaces and storage areas
- Forgetting to clean light fixtures and fans
- Inadequate carpet or floor treatment

**Safety Shortcuts:**
- Skipping required safety inspections
- Using non-compliant smoke detectors
- Ignoring electrical or plumbing issues
- Inadequate security measures
- Missing required disclosures or documentation

**Documentation Gaps:**
- Incomplete move-in inspection reports
- Missing required legal disclosures
- Outdated lease agreements or addendums
- Inadequate photo documentation
- Poor record-keeping systems
